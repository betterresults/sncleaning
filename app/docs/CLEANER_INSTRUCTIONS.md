# SN Cleaning Mobile App - Installation & Usage Guide

**For Cleaners**

This guide will help you install and use the SN Cleaning mobile app on your Android phone. The app makes it much easier to upload photos from your bookings.

---

## üì± Installation Guide (One-Time Setup)

### Step 1: Download the App

Your manager will send you a download link. Click on the link to download the `app-debug.apk` file to your phone.

### Step 2: Enable Installation

Before you can install the app, you need to allow your phone to install apps from sources other than the Play Store:

#### For Most Android Phones:
1. When you try to install the APK, you'll see a message about "Install unknown apps"
2. Tap **Settings**
3. Toggle **Allow from this source** ON
4. Go back and tap **Install**

#### For Samsung Phones:
1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Apps**
2. Tap the three dots menu (‚ãÆ) ‚Üí **Special access**
3. Tap **Install unknown apps**
4. Select your browser (Chrome, Firefox, etc.)
5. Toggle **Allow from this source** ON

### Step 3: Install & Open

1. Find the downloaded APK file (usually in **Downloads** folder or notification)
2. Tap on it to install
3. Tap **Install**
4. Once installed, tap **Open**
5. Log in with your cleaner credentials (same as the website)

‚úÖ **Done!** The app is now installed on your phone.

## üì∏ How to Use the App

### Uploading Photos for Bookings

This is the main feature that makes the app better than the website:

1. **Open the app** and log in
2. **Go to your bookings** (Bookings tab)
3. **Select a booking** that needs photos
4. **Tap "Upload Photos"** button
5. **Select photo type:**
   - Before Photos
   - After Photos
   - During Photos
6. **Choose multiple photos at once:**
   - Your phone's native photo picker will open
   - Select as many photos as you need
   - Tap **Done** or **Select**
7. **Photos upload automatically:**
   - You'll see upload progress for each photo
   - Photos are uploaded in batches (5 at a time)
   - You can see which photos succeeded or failed
8. **Stay on the screen** until all photos finish uploading

### Tips for Best Results

‚úÖ **Use WiFi when possible** - Uploading many photos uses data  
‚úÖ **Keep the app open** - Don't switch apps while uploading  
‚úÖ **Check the progress** - Make sure all photos show "‚úì Uploaded"  
‚úÖ **Retry failed uploads** - If a photo fails, you can try again

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Problem: App Won't Install

**Solution:**
- Make sure "Install unknown apps" is enabled (see Step 2 above)
- Try downloading the APK again (file might be corrupted)
- Check if your Android version is 7.0 or higher

### Problem: Camera/Photos Not Working

**Solution:**
1. Grant permissions when the app asks
2. Or go to: **Settings** ‚Üí **Apps** ‚Üí **SN Cleaning** ‚Üí **Permissions**
3. Enable **Camera** and **Storage** permissions

### Problem: Photos Not Uploading

**Solution:**
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure you're logged in
- Try uploading fewer photos at once (split into multiple batches)
- Close and reopen the app

### Problem: Can't Log In

**Solution:**
- Make sure you're using the correct credentials (same as website)
- Check your internet connection
- Contact your manager if you forgot your password
