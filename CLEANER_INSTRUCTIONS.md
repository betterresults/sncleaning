# SN Cleaning App - Installation Guide for Cleaners

## What is this?

This is the **SN Cleaning mobile app** - a native Android app that makes uploading photos much easier and more reliable than the website.

---

## Installation (One-Time Setup)

### Step 1: Download the App

1. Open the download link we sent you (in Chrome, Firefox, or your browser)
2. The file is called **app-debug.apk** (about 5-10 MB)
3. Tap **Download** when asked

### Step 2: Allow Installation

Your phone will warn you because the app is not from Google Play Store. This is normal and safe!

**On most Android phones:**
1. When you tap the downloaded APK, you'll see a warning
2. Tap **Settings** or **Install anyway**
3. Enable **"Install unknown apps"** for your browser
4. Go back and tap the APK again
5. Tap **Install**

**On Samsung phones:**
1. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Apps**
2. Find your browser (Chrome/Firefox)
3. Tap **"Install unknown apps"**
4. Enable it
5. Go back to Downloads and tap the APK

### Step 3: Open the App

1. Once installed, tap **Open** or find the app icon on your home screen
2. Login with your cleaner email and password (same as the website)

1. **Open a booking** from your list

6. **Photos upload automatically** in batches
7. **Watch the progress bar** - you'll see how many are done

## Troubleshooting

### Can't install the APK?
- Make sure you enabled "Unknown sources" or "Install unknown apps"
- Try downloading again (file might be corrupted)
- Check you have enough storage space (need ~50 MB free)

### Camera not working?
- Go to **Settings** â†’ **Apps** â†’ **SN Cleaning**
- Tap **Permissions**
- Enable **Camera** and **Storage**

### Photos not uploading?
- Check your internet connection (WiFi recommended)
- Make sure you granted Storage permission
- Try closing and reopening the app
- Contact support if issue persists

### App says "Login failed"?
- Use the same email and password as the website
- Check your internet connection
- Contact admin if you forgot your password

## Why Native App vs Website?

The website (PWA) works well, but some Android phones struggle with:
- Selecting many photos at once
- Camera permissions
- Memory management with large files

The native app solves these issues by using Android's built-in file picker and better memory handling.

**If you prefer the website**, you can still use it! Just open your browser and go to the normal website URL. Both work!
